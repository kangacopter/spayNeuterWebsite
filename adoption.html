adopting a pet can save a life.

In addition to spaying and neutering so you do not contribute to the pet population, you can help out by adopting! By adopting you are keeping one more pet out of the shelter, so consider doing so instead of buying from a pet store or a breeder. Also, as fun as kittens and puppies can be- sometimes an older, trained pet is a better fit for your and your family.

where to adopt.

There are many options for where you can go to adopt your new pet. Nationwide, there are about 3,500 animal shelters. Local animal shelters are obvious choices, but did you know that some organizations operate out of pet stores? You can also use websites like Petfinder.com to help you find your new friend.
